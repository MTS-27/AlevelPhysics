PMT is your main resource
physicsandmathstutor.com has AQA past paper questions sorted by topic. This is the best revision tool — do questions by topic until the last 2 weeks. Use A Level Physics Online for video explainers.
Master the formula sheet
Know exactly what's in your AQA data booklet. Stop memorising things that are given to you, and focus on those that aren't (wave speed eq, de Broglie, specific electrical formulas).
Paper 3 Section A = Free Marks
Tests practical skills — plotting graphs, identifying errors, improvements. Practice drawing best-fit lines with error bars. Learn keywords like "anomalous result", "systematic error".
Respect Fields (Topic 7)
Gravitational, electric, and magnetic fields naturally follow similar patterns. Capacitors and EM induction especially need worked examples, not just notes. Allocate extra time here.
Show EVERY step
AQA gives method marks. Even with a wrong final answer, you can get 2/3 marks by writing the correct formula and substituting values. "By inspection" skips cost marks!
